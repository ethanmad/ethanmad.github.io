Ready to #HyperType?

HyperTyper is the easiest way to instantly improve your typing abilities. Whether you're looking to improve your average score on a typing competition website, dominate your opponents in a typing race, or get the top spot on a speed typing website, HyperTyper is the utility you need.

To prepare your computer, please run the following in a *nix terminal

$ yes '#HyperType'
$ yes HyperTyper
$ sudo su HyperTypeMe

Just kidding. HyperTypeMe isn't a command... yet. And those yeses just print out '#HyperType' and 'HyperTyper'. Crtl + C to end those.

Installation

If you're on Chrome, get the HyperTyper Chrome extension. Otherwise, add a bookmarklet from script.js.
